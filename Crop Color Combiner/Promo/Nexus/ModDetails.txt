Overview:
Adds two keybinds -- one to unify all color variants of items in your inventory to the default color and another to reduce the quality of items in your inventory to no stars. You can target just flowers or all item types and customize the key assignments for both.

Details:
[b]Description:

[color=#ffff00]Crop Color Combiner[/color][/b] is a SMAPI mod which adds two keybinds -- one to unify all color variants of items in your inventory to the default color and another to reduce the quality of items in your inventory to no stars. You can target just flowers or all item types and customize the key assignments for both in the [i]config.json[/i] file; this file is not included with the download and will be automatically created the first time the game is run with the mod loaded.


[b]Installation:
[/b]
[list=1]
[*]Install the latest version of [url=https://smapi.io/]SMAPI[/url].
[*]Unzip the download and place the included [color=#b6d7a8][code]CropColorCombiner[/code][/color] folder into your [color=#b6d7a8][code]Mods[/code][/color] folder.
[/list]

[b]Configuration:
[/b]
[list]
[*][color=#6d9eeb][b]Unify_Color_Keybind[/b][/color] lets you choose which key to use to trigger color unification. There is a [url=https://stardewvalleywiki.com/Modding:Player_Guide/Key_Bindings]list of valid key codes[/url] on the wiki. The default is [color=#b4a7d6][i]OemOpenBrackets[/i] [/color]("[color=#b4a7d6] [ [/color]") and the keybind can be disabled by setting it to [color=#b4a7d6][i]None[/i][/color].
[*][color=#6d9eeb][b]Unify_Color_For_Flowers[/b][/color] lets you choose whether the color unification will affect flowers. Choices are [color=#b4a7d6][i]true[/i] [/color](default) or [i][color=#b4a7d6]false[/color][/i].
[*][color=#6d9eeb][b]Unify_Color_For_Everything_Else[/b][/color] lets you choose whether the color unification will affect all other object types. Choices are [color=#b4a7d6][i]true[/i] [/color]or [i][color=#b4a7d6]false[/color][/i] (default).
[*][color=#6d9eeb][b]Reduce_Quality_Keybind[/b][/color] lets you choose which key to use to trigger quality reduction. There is a [url=https://stardewvalleywiki.com/Modding:Player_Guide/Key_Bindings]list of valid key codes[/url] on the wiki. The default is [color=#b4a7d6][i]OemCloseBrackets[/i] [/color]("[color=#b4a7d6] ] [/color]") and the keybind can be disabled by setting it to [color=#b4a7d6][i]None[/i][/color].
[*][color=#6d9eeb][b]Reduce_Quality_For_Flowers[/b][/color] lets you choose whether the quality reduction will affect flowers. Choices are [color=#b4a7d6][i]true[/i] [/color](default) or [i][color=#b4a7d6]false[/color][/i].
[*][color=#6d9eeb][b]Reduce_Quality_For_Everything_Else[/b][/color] lets you choose whether the quality reduction will affect all other object types. Choices are [color=#b4a7d6][i]true[/i] [/color]or [i][color=#b4a7d6]false[/color][/i] (default).
[/list]
