Overview:
Seasonal Tub o Flowers is a Content Patcher mod which gives the Tub o' Flowers a unique texture for every season with flowers specific to that season.

Details:
[b]Description:

[color=#ffff00]Seasonal Tub o Flowers[/color][/b] is a Content Patcher mod which gives the Tub o' Flowers a unique texture for every season with flowers specific to that season.


[b]Installation:[/b]
[list=1]
[*]Install the latest version of [url=https://smapi.io/]SMAPI[/url].
[*]Install the latest version of [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/stardewvalley/mods/1915]Content Patcher[/url].
[*]Unzip the download and place the included [color=#b6d7a8][code][CP] Seasonal Tub o Flowers[/code][/color] folder into your [color=#b6d7a8][code]Mods[/code][/color] folder.
[/list]


[b]Configuration:
[/b]
[list]
[*][color=#6d9eeb][b]WinterVersion[/b][/color] lets you choose how the Tub looks in Winter. Choices are [color=#b4a7d6][i]flowers[/i] [/color](default) or [i][color=#b4a7d6]empty[/color][/i].
[/list]



[b]Compatibility:[/b]

[b][color=#ffff00]Seasonal Tub o Flowers[/color][/b] requires [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/stardewvalley/mods/1915]Content Patcher[/url] version 1.3 or higher (which means it also requires [url=https://smapi.io]SMAPI[/url] version 2.6 or higher). It should work with both version 1.2 and 1.3 of Stardew Valley, but it has only been tested on version 1.3
